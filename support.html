Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → tap any locked feature → tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the paywall.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → BearPass → Cancel Subscription.

Does BearPass work offline?

Yes. All questions and content work fully offline after the first launch.

Which DMV test does BearPass cover?

BearPass covers the California DMV written knowledge test for passenger vehicles.
