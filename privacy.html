Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

BearPass ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the BearPass iOS app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app stores your quiz progress and settings locally on your device only.

We use RevenueCat to process in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. You can review their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases

BearPass offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Weekly, Monthly, 3-Month). Payments are processed by Apple via your App Store account. We do not store any payment information.

Data Storage

All app data (test progress, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

BearPass is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
